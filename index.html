Process

How It Works

Three simple steps from breakdown to repair.

Report

Driver reports a breakdown from the mobile app with GPS location and photos.

Match

FixiTruck broadcasts to nearby shops who review details and send repair offers.

Repair

Driver accepts the best offer. Shop completes the repair and sends the invoice.
